    About us

    Our History

    2019 - Opening

    During the peak of the global pandemic, our team launched HI-Toyz as an online toy reseller and dropshipper. Our vision was to spread joy to every corner of the earth.

    2021 - First Store

    HI-Toyz opened its first onsite store, offering a space for customers to explore toys, interact with staff, and experience the joy of play. The store's atmosphere and curated selection fostered a sense of community.

    2022 - Second Store

    In 2022, HI-Toyz launched its second store, expanding its reach to cater to more toy enthusiasts. This new location replicated the success of the first store, captivating customers with a curated selection and dedicated staff.

    2020 - First Warehouse

    In 2020, HI-Toyz purchased its first warehouse to meet the growing demand for our toys. This allowed us to streamline operations, store inventory efficiently, and fulfill orders promptly.

    2021 - Rise in popularity

    HI-Toyz's growing popularity led to an overcrowded store, surpassing expectations as eager customers flocked to explore the wide array of toys and experience the joy it offered.

    2023 - Third Store

    Continuing its growth, HI-Toyz proudly opened its third store in 2023. This milestone furthered its mission to bring joy worldwide, reaching new communities and solidifying its position as a top toy retailer.
